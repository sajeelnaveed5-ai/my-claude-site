Ally Impex is a leading USA-based wholesale manufacturer specializing in premium sportswear, activewear, athletic uniforms, and fashion apparel. We serve both B2B clients — including retailers, gyms, sports brands, and corporate buyers — as well as B2C wholesale customers seeking bulk orders at competitive prices.

Our vertically integrated supply chain ensures the highest quality control, from raw material sourcing to finished goods. Every stitch, seam, and fabric selection reflects our commitment to performance and style.

With custom branding, OEM/ODM services, and flexible MOQ starting at just 50 units per style, we make wholesale accessible for businesses of every size.
